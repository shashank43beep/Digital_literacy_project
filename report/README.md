For this task, I used Canva to create a digital literacy awareness infographic. The design highlights key aspects such as the meaning of digital literacy, useful tools, safe internet practices, and professional online behavior. I selected a clean template and organized the content into sections for better clarity.

One interesting part of this task was exploring how visual design can make information more engaging and easier to understand. The challenging part was selecting the most relevant points and fitting them into a limited space without making the design cluttered.
